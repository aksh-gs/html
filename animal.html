Protect Animals

Wildlife is very important for human life. Wildlife and ecology play an important role in sustaining life on our planet. India and our world are rich in green and green wildlife. However, there has been a massive decline in wildlife from our planet. Many animals and birds are on the verge of extinction from our world today. Some of the reasons for the extinction of wildlife are due to disturbance of ecological balance and human greed and selfishness. There is a dearth of wildlife habitat to meet the needs of the people by cutting down the forest. So it is a matter of deep concern to us now. We all need to take urgent action to protect wildlife. We need to take immediate action to prevent deforestation. We are not all complying with wildlife protection laws. This wildlife protection law was introduced in 1972.

About As

Wildlife is a precious gift from God for this planet. The term "fauna" is not only for wild animals but also for all non-domesticated life forms including birds, insects, plants, fungi and even microscopic organisms. To maintain a healthy ecological balance on this land, animals, plants and marine species are as important as humans. Every organism on this earth has a unique place in the food chain that contributes to the ecosystem in its own way. But, unfortunately today, many animals and birds are endangered.
